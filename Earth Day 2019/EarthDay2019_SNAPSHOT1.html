title

Earth day 2019

A day to celebrate our wonderful planet, and help preserve it for the future

Intro

Earth is the planet we live on. It is the only planet in our solar system that we know has life on it. Earth is abundant with plants, animals, water, and is the only planet that is sustainable for life (that we know of)

What you can do to help

Unfortunately, our beautiful planet is under attack by threats like radiation, global warming, overpopulation, deforestation, fracking, and many other threats. We need to preserve this planet!

Helping can be very simple. If everyone were to pitch in, we could make a big difference.

* For every plant you take down, plant one in its place, along with another plant

* Pick up garbage. This can help

* Don't start any unneccesary fires

* Conserve energy. Turn off the lights when you aren't using them

* Find other ways to help the planet

Live on, Earth!
