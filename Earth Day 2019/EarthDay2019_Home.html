Earth day 2019

A day to celebrate our wonderful planet, and help preserve it for the future

Intro

Earth is the planet we live on. It is the only planet in our solar system that we know has life on it. Earth is abundant with plants, animals, water, and is the only planet that is sustainable for life (that we know of)

Environmental issues

Our planet currently is in grave danger due to many issues. These issues include:

Overpopulation

Humans overpopulated Earth. There are not enough resources for over 7.5 billion people to survive on this planet. One of the safe ways to deal with this issue is to not have a baby

Deforestation

Rainforests are a beautiful environment with a rare ecosystem that is very difficult to imitate. There are a lot of beautiful animals that live here, along with important resources which are being taken down and destroyed. Over 20% of the worlds rainforests have been destroyed. It will take decades for them to grow back. Unfortunately, there isn't much you can do to help.

Global warming & Climate change

Global warming and climate change are HUGE and SERIOUS issues. Our planets Ozone layer has protected us from the sun for billions of years, but now it is being destroyed. As it is taking more damage, the dangers of the sun come to Earth even stronger. This can make dangerous events occur like forest fires, droughts, and cancer. Would you like to have cancer? Most likely you wouldn't. What would you do if you didn't have any water? You would die!

But what can you do to help? Technology is getting a lot better. Eventually, electric cars will become more accessible for the consumer. So if possible, try to get an electric car. The exhaust from your cars and trucks is destroying the Ozone layer, and once that is gone, Earth will go up in flames. Everything you ever worked for will be burned to nothingness right in front of your eyes. Other than that, don't start fires, don't smoke, don't output too much exhaust (a laptop is completely fine) and be more cautious on what you do

What you can do to help

Unfortunately, our beautiful planet is under attack by threats like radiation, global warming, overpopulation, deforestation, fracking, and many other threats. We need to preserve this planet!

Helping can be very simple. If everyone were to pitch in, we could make a big difference.

* For every plant you take down, plant one in its place, along with another plant

* Pick up garbage. This can help

* Don't start any unnecessary fires

* Conserve energy. Turn off the lights when you aren't using them

* Find other ways to help the planet
